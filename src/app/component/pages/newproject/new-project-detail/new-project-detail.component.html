About Sharjah Garden City

Sharjah Garden City is a project by Shoumous Properties located on the Maliha Road, Sharjah, with a completion date set as second quarter of 2019. It is a residential complex offering luxury villas with an area of 4250 Sq.Ft. Each villa consists of two floors with 5 bedrooms. Also residences feature a spacious garden area and balconies on the first floor, as well as an automatic car park with two parking spaces inside and an additional pedestrian entrance.

The community offers its residents an easy access to various restaurants, shops, leisure and recreational areas. The complex is placed on Maliha Road, which is only 3 kilometers from Emirates Road and less than 15 minutes drive from Sharjah Airport and Dubai. 

Residents will be able to enjoy following facilities:

  • Large backyard
  • Gated community
  • Landscaped surroundings
  • Mosque
  • Small mall centre
  • Kids play area
  • Multiple access routes.
